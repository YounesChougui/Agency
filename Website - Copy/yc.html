Featured Case Studies

B2B Lead Generation at Scale (Radius)

Problem: Needed to improve B2B lead quality from a £4.5M annual budget across diverse European markets, moving beyond simple volume metrics.

Action: Implemented automated budget pacing, refined audience segmentation, localized landing pages, and integrated Google Analytics with Salesforce for end-to-end attribution.

Result: Significantly optimized CPL and CVR. Collaboration with sales ensured inbound leads were sales-ready, demonstrating clear impact on full-funnel performance.

B2C E-commerce Growth (CSW)

Problem: Drive significant e-commerce growth for a gaming computer business on a limited £15k budget in a competitive market.

Action: Executed a multi-channel strategy (Social, Email, SEO, PPC) based on competitor research. Created tailored content and optimized product descriptions.

Result: Achieved a 30% increase in lead generation, a 5% improvement in conversion rates, and a 30% boost in overall website traffic.

SEO & Social Media Growth (Zuyu)

Problem: Elevate brand presence and drive meaningful engagement across Instagram, Facebook, and LinkedIn, moving beyond vanity metrics.

Action: Designed a ground-up social media strategy with daily content, strategic scheduling, and targeted ad campaigns. Focused on active community engagement.

Result: In 6 months, boosted visibility by 25%, follower growth by 35%, and engagement by 40%. Ads saw an 18% CTR improvement and a 12% CPC reduction.
